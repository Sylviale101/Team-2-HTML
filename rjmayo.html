What is a JDM car?

Definition:

JDM stands for the "Japanese Domestic Market" and practically applies to all kinds of imported goods sold from Japan. In this case, we'll be applying the definition to cars only.

Culture:

Many car enthusiasts target their interests into Japanese cars only, because of they can be easily modified for speed, looks, or practicality. The fandom of JDM cars has heavily increased recently with the romanticism of the lifestyle surrounding vintage JDM cars.

Racing:

The majority of these cars have a heritage in racing and were built for speed. Plenty of these enthusaists specifically choose JDM cars for a simple platform to build off of, and for the strength of their engines.
